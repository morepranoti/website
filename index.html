About Us

Vision:

To be a leading institution that provides a transformative education to create leaders and Innovators, and generates new knowledge for society and industry.

Mission:

  • To provide an education that transforms students through
    rigorous coursework and by providing an understanding of the needs of society and industry.
  • To encourage innovative thinking and creativity and inculcate research skills in our students.
  • To optimize the use of available infrastructure for sustained development of the college.
  • To supplement and enrich the existing curriculum through seminars and extension lectures by eminent scholars.

Values:

  • Each person is treated with respect and dignity in all situations. Criticize only ideas not people, and share responsibility.
  • College fosters a learning environment that promotes responsible, principled behavior which respects the dignity of all members of the community.
  • Provide educational programs that lead to the acquisition of knowledge and skills necessary to achieve information literacy, career advancement, personal enrichment, leadership and service to the community.

Our Programs

Bachelor of Business Administration

A Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a bachelor's degree that helps you establish a fundamental understanding of business and how various aspects of it apply to the real world.

Bachelor of Computer Application

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a three-year degree program that focuses on computer applications computer science, software engineering and software development.

B. Sc. (Computer Science)

BSc Computer Science is a three-year undergraduate course program that lays emphasis on computer networking, and programming languages. an IT systems manager, or networking specialist, etc.

Bachelor of Journalism

B.J. is a 3-year undergraduate degree that enables you to gain knowledge and experience in the journalism and media industry. The course focuses on Radio Journalism, Broadcasting, and Photojournalism etc

M. Sc. (Computer Science)

MSc Computer Science is a two-year post-graduation program to develop advanced programming skills and strategies to solve various challenges. It focuses more on developing software and networking-based skills.
